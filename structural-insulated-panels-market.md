<p><p>Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) are commonly categorized into four main types in the market. EPS Panels, PUR/PIR Panels, Glass Wool Panels, and Others. EPS Panels are made from expanded polystyrene, PUR/PIR Panels from polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foam, Glass Wool Panels from glass fibers, and Others include panels made from materials such as mineral wool or straw. Each type offers different levels of thermal performance, fire resistance, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various construction applications.</p></p>
