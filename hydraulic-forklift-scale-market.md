<p><p>Wired forklift scales are connected to the forklift through wires, allowing for real-time weight measurements during lifting operations. On the other hand, wireless forklift scales use wireless technology to transmit weight data to a display device or computer without the need for physical wires, providing more flexibility and convenience. Both types of forklift scales are designed to accurately weigh loads and improve efficiency in material handling operations. Customers can choose between wired and wireless options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
