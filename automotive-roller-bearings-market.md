Cylindrical roller bearings have high radial load capacity, while tapered roller bearings are designed to handle both radial and thrust loads. Needle roller bearings are compact in design and offer high load carrying capability. Other types of roller bearings include spherical roller bearings and thrust roller bearings, each serving specific applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
