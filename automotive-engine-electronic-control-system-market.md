<p><p>Automotive Engine Electronic Control System consists of various components such as Oxygen Sensor, EGR Valve, Catalytic Converter, Air Pump, PCV Valve, and Charcoal Canister. The Oxygen Sensor helps measure the amount of oxygen in the exhaust gases to optimize fuel efficiency. The EGR Valve recirculates exhaust gases to reduce emissions. The Catalytic Converter converts harmful pollutants into less harmful emissions. The Air Pump helps in secondary air injection for better combustion. The PCV Valve controls the flow of gases from the engine crankcase. The Charcoal Canister absorbs and stores fuel vapors.</p></p>
