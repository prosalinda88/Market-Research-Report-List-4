<p><p>Horizontal Injection Molding Machines are commonly used in the manufacturing industry for producing various plastic products through the injection molding process. Electric machines offer higher energy efficiency, faster cycle times, and better repeatability, making them a preferred choice for manufacturers looking to improve production efficiency and reduce operational costs. Moreover, the trend of customizing injection molding machines to meet specific production requirements is also gaining traction in the market.</p></p>
