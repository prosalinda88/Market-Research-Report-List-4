<p><p>Packaging testing includes various types of testing methods to ensure the durability and effectiveness of packaging materials in protecting products during transportation and storage. Drop test involves dropping packages from a certain height to simulate handling during shipping. Vibration test subjects packages to vibration to assess their ability to withstand repetitive motions. Shock test replicates sudden impacts during handling. Temperature testing checks how packaging materials hold up under extreme temperature conditions. Other tests may include compression testing, moisture resistance, and burst strength testing.</p></p>
