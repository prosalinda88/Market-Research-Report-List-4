<p><strong>The Digital Ceramic Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Printing</li><li>Dye-sublimation Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital ceramic printing machines are specialized printers used for various applications in the ceramic industry. There are two main types of digital ceramic printing machines: UV printing and dye-sublimation printing. UV printing involves using ultraviolet light to cure inks onto the ceramic surface, resulting in vibrant and durable prints. On the other hand, dye-sublimation printing involves transferring dyes onto the ceramic surface using heat, resulting in high-quality and long-lasting prints. Both types of printing offer unique benefits and cater to different needs in the ceramic industry.</p></p>
