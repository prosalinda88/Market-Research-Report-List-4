<p><ul><li>Alumina Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Silica Slurry</li><li>Ceria Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP Polishing Fluid market types include Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurries. Alumina Slurry is composed of abrasive particles of alumina that aid in the removal of material during the polishing process. Colloidal Silica Slurry contains abrasive particles of silica suspended in a liquid medium, which provide a gentle polishing action. Ceria Slurries consist of cerium oxide particles that offer high polishing efficiency for advanced semiconductor applications. Each type of polishing fluid caters to specific polishing requirements in industries such as electronics and optics.</p></p>
