<p><p>ZigBee Home Automation market consists of two main types of devices: Full Function Devices (FFDs) and Low Cost Reduced Function Endpoint Devices (RFDs). FFDs are high-end devices that act as coordinators and routers in the network, providing full functionality and features. On the other hand, RFDs are simpler, more cost-effective devices that have limited functionality and serve as endpoints in the network. Both types of devices play important roles in creating a robust and efficient ZigBee Home Automation ecosystem.</p></p>
