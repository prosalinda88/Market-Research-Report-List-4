<p><p>The standalone battery-operated residential smoke alarm market consists of three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, while ionization smoke alarms rely on ionized particles to detect smoke. Combination smoke alarms incorporate both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive smoke detection. These different types cater to varying consumer preferences and needs for residential fire safety.</p></p>
