<p><strong>The Thermochromic Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversible Thermochromic Materials</li><li>Irreversible Thermochromic Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermochromic materials are substances that change color with fluctuations in temperature. Reversible thermochromic materials revert to their original color when the temperature changes, making them ideal for applications like mood rings and thermometers. Irreversible thermochromic materials permanently change color when heated or cooled, finding use in applications like tamper-evident labels and indicator materials. Both types of thermochromic materials are widely used in various industries including textiles, packaging, and printing for their unique color-changing properties.</p></p>
