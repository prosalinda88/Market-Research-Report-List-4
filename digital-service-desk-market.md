<p><p>The Digital Service Desk market can be categorized into two main types based on their deployment method: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise service desks are installed and operated locally on the organization's own servers and infrastructure. They offer more control and customization but require higher upfront costs. Cloud-Based service desks, on the other hand, are hosted and managed by third-party providers, offering lower upfront costs, scalability, and flexibility. However, they may have limitations in terms of customization and control.</p></p>
