<p><strong>The Sustainable Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid Nonwovens</li><li>Wetlaid Nonwovens</li><li>Spunbond Nonwovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drylaid nonwovens are created by layering fibers on a production line without the use of water. This type of nonwoven is commonly used in products like wipes and geotextiles. Wetlaid nonwovens involve a similar process but use water and binders to create the final product. These nonwovens are often used in medical and filtration applications. Spunbond nonwovens are produced by extruding fibers onto a moving belt where they are bonded together. This type of nonwoven is commonly used in hygiene products and industrial applications.</p></p>
