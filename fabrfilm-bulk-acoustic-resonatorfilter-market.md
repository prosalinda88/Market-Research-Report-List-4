<p><p>FABRFilm Bulk Acoustic Resonator (FBAR) Filters come in two main types - Membrane Type and Airgap Type. </p><p>Membrane Type filters utilize a thin, flexible membrane that vibrates at a specific frequency to filter out unwanted signals. This type of filter is known for its high selectivity and low insertion loss.</p><p>Airgap Type filters use an airgap between two conductive layers to create an acoustic wave that filters out specific frequencies. This type of filter is known for its high Q factor and excellent temperature stability.</p></p>
