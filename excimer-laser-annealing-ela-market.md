<p><p>Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA) is a process used in the semiconductor industry to improve the properties of thin films on silicon wafers. There are two main types of ELA markets based on the wavelength of the laser used: XeCl (308nm) ELA and KrF (248nm) ELA. XeCl ELA is known for its deeper penetration into the film, making it suitable for thicker layers, while KrF ELA is often preferred for finer details and smaller features. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
