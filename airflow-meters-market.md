<p><p>Airflow meters can be classified into two main types: analog and digital. Analog airflow meters utilize a mechanical mechanism to measure airflow, providing a visual representation through a dial or gauge. On the other hand, digital airflow meters use electronic sensors to measure and display airflow readings digitally. While analog airflow meters are more traditional and cost-effective, digital airflow meters offer greater accuracy and precision in measuring airflow. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
