<p><p>Guitar loopers come in two main market types: floor-based and desktop. Floor-based loopers are typically larger pedals that are controlled with footswitches, making them ideal for live performances. They often have more features and are more ruggedly built. Desktop loopers, on the other hand, are smaller units meant for use in home studios or for practicing at home. They are usually controlled with buttons or knobs and may offer fewer features than their floor-based counterparts. Both types cater to different needs of musicians, whether for live shows or home use.</p></p>
