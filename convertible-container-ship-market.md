<p><p>The Convertible Container Ship market consists of two main types: FCL Container Ships and LCL Container Ships. FCL Container Ships primarily carry full container loads which are owned by one shipper. These ships are designed to accommodate a higher volume of containers for a single customer, providing more space and efficiency. On the other hand, LCL Container Ships carry multiple smaller shipments from different shippers in one container, consolidating cargo from various sources. This allows for cost-effective and flexible shipping options for smaller businesses and individuals with less cargo volume.</p></p>
