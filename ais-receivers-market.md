<p><p>AIS (Automatic Identification System) receivers come in various types such as NMEA, Seatalk, USB, and others. USB receivers are popular for connecting AIS systems to computers or other devices. Other types of AIS receivers may include wireless or Ethernet options, providing flexibility for different installation and connectivity needs in the maritime industry.</p></p>
