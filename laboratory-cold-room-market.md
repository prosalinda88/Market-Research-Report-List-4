<p><p>Laboratory Cold Room market can be categorized into two main types: Modular Cold Room and Cold Room market. Modular Cold Rooms are prefabricated rooms that can be easily assembled and disassembled to meet specific needs. They are highly customizable and offer efficient temperature control for laboratory storage. On the other hand, Cold Rooms are standalone units that are designed to store perishable goods at a constant low temperature. Both types of cold rooms play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and integrity of sensitive products in laboratory and commercial settings.</p></p>
