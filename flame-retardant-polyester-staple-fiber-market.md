<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber (FRP) can be categorized into two main types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber is manufactured using inherently flame retardant materials during the production process, providing permanent flame retardant properties. On the other hand, Treated FRP Staple Fiber involves applying flame retardant chemicals or coatings onto the fiber after production to enhance its flame retardant capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in various industries where fire safety is a priority.</p></p>
