<p><strong>The Polyimide Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide tubes are available in two main types - Seamless Tubing and Spiral Wound Tubing. Seamless Tubing is made from a single piece of polyimide material, ensuring no weak points or seams in the structure. This type of tubing is known for its excellent strength and resistance to high temperatures. Spiral Wound Tubing, on the other hand, is created by wrapping multiple layers of polyimide material in a spiral pattern. This design allows for flexibility and ease of installation in various applications.</p></p>
