Transcutaneous bilirubin meters are portable devices that are placed against the baby's skin to measure bilirubin levels non-invasively. Bench-top bilirubin meters are larger, stationary devices used in clinical settings for more accurate and detailed measurements. Both types of meters provide valuable information to healthcare professionals for monitoring and managing infant jaundice.</p></p>
