<p><strong>The Resistance Furnace Power Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Voltage Regulation</li><li>Indirect Voltage Regulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance furnace power transformers are used in industry to regulate voltage for electric resistance furnaces. In direct voltage regulation, the transformer adjusts the voltage directly at the output terminals, ensuring a consistent voltage supply to the furnace. In indirect voltage regulation, the transformer regulates the voltage by adjusting the input current, which in turn affects the output voltage. Both types of transformers play a crucial role in maintaining stable and efficient operation of resistance furnaces in various industrial applications.</p></p>
