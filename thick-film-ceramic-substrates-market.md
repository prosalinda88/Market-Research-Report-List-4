<p><ul><li>Single-layer Thick Film Ceramic Substrates</li><li>Multilayer Thick Film Ceramic Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick Film Ceramic Substrates are available in two main types: single-layer and multilayer. Single-layer substrates are made of a single ceramic layer with thick film technology applied to it, suitable for simple electronic applications. Multilayer substrates consist of multiple ceramic layers with thick film technology, allowing for more complex and advanced electronic circuit designs. Single-layer substrates are more cost-effective and easier to manufacture, while multilayer substrates offer higher performance and density for sophisticated electronic devices.</p></p>
