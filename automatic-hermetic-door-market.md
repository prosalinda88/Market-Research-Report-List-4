<p><p>The automatic hermetic door market includes two main types: single leaf and double leaves. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel that opens and closes automatically to provide a hermetic seal. Double leaves doors, on the other hand, are made up of two panels that open in the middle and close together to create a hermetic seal. Both types are commonly used in hospitals, laboratories, and other facilities where maintaining a controlled environment is essential.</p></p>
