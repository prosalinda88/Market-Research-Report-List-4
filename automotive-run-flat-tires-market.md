<p><strong>The Automotive Run-flat Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Technology</li><li>Self-sealing Technology</li><li>Auxiliary-supported Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive run-flat tires come in three main types based on their technologies: self-supporting technology, self-sealing technology, and auxiliary-supported technology. Self-supporting technology allows the tire to operate without air pressure, ensuring continued mobility even after a puncture. Self-sealing technology features a special lining that can seal small punctures to prevent air loss. Auxiliary-supported technology involves a supportive ring inside the tire that provides structural support in case of a blowout, allowing the vehicle to continue driving safely.</p></p>
