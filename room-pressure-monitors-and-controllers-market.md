<p><p>Room pressure monitors and controllers come in two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed room pressure monitors are permanently installed in a specific location within a facility to continuously monitor and control pressure levels. These are typically used in critical environments such as hospital operating rooms or pharmaceutical clean rooms. Portable room pressure monitors are designed to be easily moved from room to room to quickly assess pressure levels and ensure compliance with safety standards. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining a safe and controlled environment.</p></p>
