<p><p>Impulse heat sealers are used to seal plastic bags and other materials using heat. There are two main types: foot impulse heat sealers and hand impulse heat sealers. Foot impulse heat sealers are operated by foot pedal, allowing for hands-free operation, ideal for sealing large volumes of bags. Hand impulse heat sealers are operated manually by hand, making them more portable and suitable for sealing smaller quantities of bags. Both types are widely used in industries such as food packaging, medical packaging, and manufacturing.</p></p>
