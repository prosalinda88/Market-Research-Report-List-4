<p><p>Metal processing lubricants are essential for enhancing the efficiency and durability of machinery during various processes. Quenching lubricants are used to rapidly cool hot metal parts, preventing distortion and improving hardness. Cutting and grinding lubricants help in reducing friction and heat between the tool and workpiece, increasing machining accuracy and prolonging tool life. Corrosion preventives help in protecting metal surfaces from deteriorating due to exposure to moisture and chemicals. Each type of lubricant serves a specific purpose in the metal processing industry.</p></p>
