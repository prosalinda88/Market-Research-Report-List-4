Cellulose triacetate fiber is another type of manmade cellulosic fiber that is similar to cellulose acetate, but with an additional acetyl group. It is known for its high heat resistance and wrinkle-resistance properties. Both fibers are widely used in the textile industry for clothing and home furnishings.</p></p>
