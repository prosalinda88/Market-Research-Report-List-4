<p><p>Lifting columns are commonly categorized into multi-stage and two-stage lifting columns. On the other hand, two-stage lifting columns have a simpler design with two sections for height adjustment, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution for applications that do not require as much height variability. Both types of lifting columns offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
