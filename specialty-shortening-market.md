<p><p>Specialty shortening market can be divided into two main types: plant-based specialty shortening and animal-derived specialty shortening. Plant-based specialty shortening is derived from sources such as palm oil, coconut oil, or sunflower oil, making it a popular choice among consumers looking for vegan or sustainable options. On the other hand, animal-derived specialty shortening is made from animal fats like lard or tallow, often preferred for its unique flavor profile in certain recipes. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and dietary restrictions in the market.</p></p>
