<p><p>Steel market is broadly categorized into two types: Carbon Steel and Alloy Steel. Carbon Steel is primarily composed of iron and carbon, with small amounts of other elements. It is more cost-effective and commonly used in construction and manufacturing industries. On the other hand, Alloy Steel contains additional elements like nickel, chromium, and manganese to enhance its properties like strength, toughness, and corrosion resistance. It is used in specialized applications such as aerospace, automotive, and engineering. Both types play crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
