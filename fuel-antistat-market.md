<p><ul><li>Metal Antistatic Agent</li><li>Non-Metallic Antistatic Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel antistat market is segmented into two main types: metal antistatic agents and non-metallic antistatic agents. Metal antistatic agents typically use conductive metals like copper or aluminum to dissipate static electricity in fuel storage and transportation systems. Non-metallic antistatic agents, on the other hand, rely on additives like surfactants or organic molecules to reduce the buildup of static charge. Both types of antistatic agents are essential in preventing dangerous sparks that could lead to fires or explosions in fuel handling processes.</p></p>
