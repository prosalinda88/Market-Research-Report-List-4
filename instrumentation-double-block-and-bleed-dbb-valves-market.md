<p><p>Instrumentation Double Block and Bleed (DBB) Valves are designed to isolate and bleed fluid in a pipeline. Single Block Valves provide a single isolation point, while Single Block and Bleed Valves offer an additional bleed port. Double Block Valves feature two separate isolation points, offering enhanced safety. Double Block and Bleed Valves include an additional bleed valve between the two isolation points, providing a complete isolation solution. Each type of valve offers varying levels of isolation and safety, catering to different applications and industry requirements.</p></p>
