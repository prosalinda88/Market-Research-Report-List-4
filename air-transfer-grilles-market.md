<p><p>Air transfer grilles are classified into two main types based on their design - non-vision style and vision style. Non-vision style air transfer grilles are solid panels with no openings, providing complete privacy and security. On the other hand, vision style air transfer grilles have slotted or perforated designs that allow for visibility and air flow between rooms. Both types serve the purpose of facilitating air transfer while offering different levels of privacy and aesthetics to suit varying preferences and requirements.</p></p>
