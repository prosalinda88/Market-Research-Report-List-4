<p><p>Industrial oxygen market can be divided into three main types based on delivery methods: On-Site Type, Bulk Type, and Cylinder Type. On-Site Type involves producing oxygen directly at the industrial facility using oxygen generation systems. Bulk Type involves delivering large quantities of oxygen in cryogenic liquid form via tankers. Cylinder Type involves supplying oxygen in compressed gas form stored in cylinders. Each type caters to different industrial needs and requirements, offering flexibility and convenience in oxygen supply for various applications.</p></p>
