Dual diodes, on the other hand, have two separate anode/cathode pairs in a single package, allowing for more complex circuit designs and applications. Both types of diodes offer high efficiency, fast switching speeds, and low forward voltage drop, making them ideal for power electronics and renewable energy applications.</p></p>
