<p><ul><li>Floatless Level Indicators</li><li>Floating Pole Type Water Level Indicators</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water level indicators are classified into three main types. Floatless level indicators use sensors to detect the water level without the need for a physical float. Floating pole type indicators consist of a float attached to a pole that moves up and down with the water level. Other types of water level indicators include ultrasonic sensors, pressure transducers, and conductive probes. Each type of indicator has its own unique features and applications, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
