<p><strong>The Companion Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunohistochemistry</li><li>Molecular Diagnosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Companion Diagnostic market includes two main types: Immunohistochemistry and Molecular Diagnosis. Immunohistochemistry involves the use of antibodies to detect specific proteins in tissue samples, allowing for diagnosis and personalized treatment selection. Molecular Diagnosis, on the other hand, focuses on analyzing DNA or RNA to identify genetic mutations that may impact a patient's response to treatment. Both types of tests help healthcare providers make more informed decisions about which therapies will be most effective for individual patients.</p></p>
