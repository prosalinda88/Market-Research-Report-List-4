<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction Machinery Tires market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have ply cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better flexibility, traction, and fuel efficiency. Bias tires, on the other hand, have ply cords that run diagonally across the tire, offering better load-bearing capacity and stability. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in the construction industry. Both radial and bias tires play a crucial role in ensuring optimal performance and safety of construction machinery.</p></p>
