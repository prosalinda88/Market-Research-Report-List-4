<p><ul><li>Internal Industrial Noise Control</li><li>External Industrial Noise Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial noise control solutions are designed to reduce noise levels in industrial environments. Internal industrial noise control focuses on reducing noise within buildings and industrial machinery, while external industrial noise control targets noise emissions from industrial facilities. Internal solutions may include acoustic barriers, absorption materials, and soundproofing treatments to reduce noise within a facility. External solutions typically involve noise barriers, enclosures, and mufflers to minimize noise pollution from industrial operations. Both types of solutions aim to improve employee safety, compliance with regulations, and community relations.</p></p>
