<p><ul><li>RF Power Amplifiers (PAs)</li><li>RF Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G RF Amplifier Chip market includes RF Power Amplifiers (PAs) and RF Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs). RF PAs are used to increase the power of the signal being transmitted, enabling longer range and better coverage. RF LNAs, on the other hand, are designed to amplify weak signals with minimal added noise, improving the signal-to-noise ratio in receiving systems. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of wireless communication systems in the 5G era.</p></p>
