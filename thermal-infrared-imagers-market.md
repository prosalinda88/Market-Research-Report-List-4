<p><p>Thermal Infrared Imagers are categorized into two main types: Uncooled Thermal Imaging Imagers and Cooled Thermal Imaging Imagers. Uncooled Thermal Imaging Imagers are more cost-effective and do not require cryogenic cooling, making them less complex and more durable. On the other hand, Cooled Thermal Imaging Imagers provide higher resolution and longer detection ranges due to their ability to cool the sensor. Both types serve various industries such as military, surveillance, automotive, and medical, offering unique benefits based on specific application needs.</p></p>
