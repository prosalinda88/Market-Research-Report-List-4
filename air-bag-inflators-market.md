<p><strong>The Air Bag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflators</li><li>Stored Gas Inflators</li><li>Hybrid Inflators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air bag inflators are crucial components in air bag systems. There are three main types of inflators in the market: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly inflate the air bag during a collision. Stored gas inflators store compressed gas which is released to inflate the air bag. Hybrid inflators combine the features of both pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators for efficient and quick inflation of air bags in vehicles.</p></p>
