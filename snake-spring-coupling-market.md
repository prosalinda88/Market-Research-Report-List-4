<p><p>Snake spring couplings are classified into two types based on stiffness - Transverse Stiffness Type and Variable Stiffness Type. Transverse Stiffness Type couplings provide a constant stiffness regardless of the angle of deflection, making them suitable for applications where consistent stiffness is required. On the other hand, Variable Stiffness Type couplings offer adjustable stiffness levels, allowing for flexibility in handling different levels of torque or misalignment. These market types cater to diverse requirements in various industries by providing specific stiffness characteristics.</p></p>
