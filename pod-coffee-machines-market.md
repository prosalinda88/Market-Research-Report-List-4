<p><p>Pod coffee machines are divided into two main market types: Closed Source System and Open Source System. Closed Source System machines use proprietary pods that are specific to a single brand, limiting consumer choices. On the other hand, Open Source System machines allow consumers to use pods from multiple brands, offering a wider range of options. Closed Source System machines tend to have exclusive partnerships with coffee producers, while Open Source System machines promote competition and variety in the market.</p></p>
