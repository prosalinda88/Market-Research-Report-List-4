<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Polarizer Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Polarizers</li><li>Beam-Splitting Polarizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polarizer equipment market includes absorptive polarizers and beam-splitting polarizers. Absorptive polarizers selectively absorb light waves in different orientations, allowing only the desired polarization to pass through. These are commonly used in camera filters and LCD displays. On the other hand, beam-splitting polarizers divide incoming light into two beams with different polarizations. They are widely used in applications like optical communications and microscopy. Both types play crucial roles in various industries where controlling light polarization is essential.</p></p>
