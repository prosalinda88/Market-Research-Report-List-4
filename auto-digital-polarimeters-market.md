<p><ul><li>Full Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto digital polarimeters come in two types: full automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Full automatic polarimeters are designed to measure samples without any manual input from the user, making them ideal for high-volume testing in busy laboratories. These instruments can automatically set the measurement parameters, calibrate the instrument, and display the results without any user intervention. </p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic polarimeters require some level of manual input, such as setting the sample parameters or calibration, but still offer some automation in the measurement process. This type of polarimeter is suitable for smaller laboratories or for users who prefer some degree of control over the measurement process.</p></p>
