<p><p>Explosives detection scanners are used for security screening to detect explosives in various settings. There are different types of technologies utilized in these scanners. Gamma-ray radiography technology uses gamma rays to create images for detecting hidden explosives. X-ray radiography technology uses X-rays for similar purposes. Muon tomography technology involves the use of muons (subatomic particles) to identify anomalies in objects, including explosives. This advanced technology offers a non-intrusive method of detecting hidden threats, making it a valuable tool for security professionals.</p></p>
