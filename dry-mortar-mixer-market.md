<p><p>Dry mortar mixers are used in construction for mixing dry powders and granular materials. Single shaft dry mortar mixers have a simple design with a single rotating shaft for mixing, making them suitable for small to medium-sized projects. Double shaft dry mortar mixers have two rotating shafts that provide more intense mixing for larger projects. Both types are designed to efficiently mix materials such as cement, sand, and additives to produce high-quality mortar for various construction applications.</p></p>
