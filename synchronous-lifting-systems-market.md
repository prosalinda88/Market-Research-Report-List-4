<p><p>Synchronous lifting systems are commonly available in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic systems require minimal human intervention as they can lift and lower loads automatically with preset controls. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems involve some level of manual operation, such as activating the lifting process or controlling the speed. Both types offer efficiency and precision in lifting heavy loads, but fully automatic systems are more suitable for tasks where minimal human intervention is desired, while semi-automatic systems provide more control to the operator.</p></p>
