Stage I NEC is characterized by mild symptoms such as feeding intolerance and mild abdominal distension. Stage II NEC involves more severe symptoms like abdominal tenderness and bloody stools. Stage III NEC is the most critical stage, with symptoms such as bowel perforation, sepsis, and metabolic acidosis.
