<p><p>Perception sensors for UAVs play a crucial role in providing autonomous navigation and obstacle avoidance capabilities. There are three main types of perception sensors used in UAVs: </p><p>1. Radar sensors emit radio waves to detect obstacles and provide information on their distance, velocity, and size. Each sensor type has its strengths and applications in UAV technology.</p></p>
