<p><p>Macro hardness testers are classified into three types based on the level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and full-automatic. Manual testers require the operator to apply the test force manually, making them suitable for low-volume testing. Semi-automatic testers automate the application of test forces but still require manual loading and unloading of samples. Full-automatic testers are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume testing. Each type of hardness tester offers varying levels of efficiency, accuracy, and ease of use to meet different testing requirements.</p></p>
