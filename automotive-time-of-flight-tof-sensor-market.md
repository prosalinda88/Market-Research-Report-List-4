<p><strong>The Automotive Time of Flight ToF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Time of Flight (ToF) sensors can be classified into two main types: Direct ToF Sensors and Indirect ToF Sensors. Direct ToF Sensors measure the time it takes for a light pulse to reflect off an object and return to the sensor, providing accurate distance measurements. Indirect ToF Sensors use additional calculations to determine distance based on factors such as phase shifting or triangulation. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in automotive applications by enabling accurate and reliable detection of objects for collision avoidance and autonomous driving systems.</p></p>
