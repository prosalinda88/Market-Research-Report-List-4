<p><p>Core drills for construction are typically categorized into two main types: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills are designed to be used without water and are ideal for situations where water cannot be used, such as indoors or in areas with limited access to water sources. Wet core drills, on the other hand, use water to lubricate the cutting surfaces and reduce friction, resulting in smoother and faster drilling. Both types of core drills cater to different market needs and preferences within the construction industry.</p></p>
