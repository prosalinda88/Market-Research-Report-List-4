<p><p>PM2.5 Monitoring Equipment Market includes various types of monitors such as TEOM Monitor, Beta Attenuation Monitor, and Others. TEOM Monitor measures the mass concentration of particulate matter using a tapered element oscillating microbalance. Beta Attenuation Monitor utilizes the attenuation of beta radiation to determine the concentration of PM2.5 particles. Other types of monitors may include optical particle counters or gravimetric samplers. Each type of monitor has its own strengths and limitations in monitoring PM2.5 levels in the atmosphere.</p></p>
