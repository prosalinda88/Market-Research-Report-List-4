<p><p>Foam fire suppression systems are categorized into three types based on their expansion capabilities: low expansion foam extinguishing system, medium expansion foam extinguishing system, and high expansion foam extinguishing system. Low expansion systems use a minimal amount of air bubbles to cover a larger surface area, ideal for enclosed spaces. Medium expansion systems create a foam blanket that rapidly expands to suppress large fires. High expansion systems produce a high volume of foam with increased coverage, best suited for open areas with high airflow.</p></p>
