<p><ul><li>Straddle Type</li><li>Suspended Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monorail systems are divided into two main types based on their design and operation - straddle type and suspended type. Straddle-type monorail systems are supported by columns or beams located above the track, while suspended type monorail systems are hung from an overhead structure. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different requirements in terms of cost, space efficiency, and capacity. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as the environment, budget, and specific needs of the project.</p></p>
