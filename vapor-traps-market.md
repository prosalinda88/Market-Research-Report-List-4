<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Vapor Traps</li><li>Cold Vapor Traps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapor traps are devices used to remove harmful vapors and gases from the air. Activated carbon vapor traps utilize activated carbon to adsorb and trap vapors before releasing clean air. Cold vapor traps use low temperatures to condense and remove vapors from the air, making them an effective solution for volatile organic compounds. Both types of vapor traps are popular in industries such as manufacturing, laboratories, and healthcare facilities where controlling air quality is crucial.</p></p>
