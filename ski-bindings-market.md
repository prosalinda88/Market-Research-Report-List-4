<p><p>Ski bindings market can be broadly categorized into three types - Strap-in Type, Step-in Type, and other variations. Strap-in bindings require the skier to manually adjust and tighten the straps to secure their boots to the skis. Step-in bindings, on the other hand, allow the skier to simply step into the bindings and click into place. Other variations may include hybrid bindings that combine features of both types or specialty bindings designed for specific types of skiing such as backcountry or racing.</p></p>
