<p><ul><li>Single Rolls</li><li>Dual Rolls</li><li>Four Rolls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary shredders come in three main types: single rolls, dual rolls, and four rolls. Single roll shredders have one rotating shaft with blades, making them ideal for small-scale shredding tasks. Dual roll shredders have two shafts with blades, allowing for increased efficiency and the ability to handle larger volumes of materials. Four roll shredders have four shafts with blades, providing even greater shredding power and versatility for handling a wide range of materials. Each type of shredder is suited for different shredding needs based on the volume and type of materials being processed.</p></p>
