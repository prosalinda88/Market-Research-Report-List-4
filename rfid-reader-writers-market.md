<p><p>RFID Reader-Writers are available in different types based on the frequency they operate at - LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and MW (Microwave). LF RFID Reader-Writers are commonly used for access control, animal tracking, and car immobilization. HF RFID Reader-Writers are used for payment systems, ticketing, and data exchange. UHF RFID Reader-Writers are suitable for supply chain management, inventory tracking, and toll collection. MW RFID Reader-Writers are used for high-speed processes like vehicle tracking and automated toll systems.</p></p>
