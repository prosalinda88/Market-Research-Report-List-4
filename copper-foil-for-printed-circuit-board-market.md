<p><p>Copper foil for printed circuit board (PCB) market consists of two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is made by passing a copper sheet through a series of rollers to reduce its thickness and ensure uniformity. Electrolytic copper foil is produced by electrolysis, resulting in a high purity copper foil with excellent electrical conductivity. Both types of copper foil are crucial components in manufacturing PCBs for various electronic devices.</p></p>
