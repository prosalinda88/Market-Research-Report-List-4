<p><ul><li>Automatic Expansion Valves without Bypass</li><li>Automatic Expansion Valves with Bypass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Expansion Valves (AEVs) without bypass are designed to regulate the flow of refrigerant through the system without allowing any excess refrigerant to bypass the valve. On the other hand, AEVs with bypass have an additional feature that allows some refrigerant to bypass the valve, providing better control over the evaporator temperature. Both types of AEVs are commonly used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to ensure proper operation and efficient cooling performance.</p></p>
