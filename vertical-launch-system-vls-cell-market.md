<p><p>The Vertical Launch System (VLS) Cell market includes various types such as Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch involves igniting the missile inside the cell before it is ejected, providing a faster response time. Cold Launch uses gas pressure to push the missile out of the cell, reducing the risk of damage to the surrounding area. Concentric Canister Launch involves storing multiple missiles concentrically in a single canister for efficient use of space and increased firepower.</p></p>
