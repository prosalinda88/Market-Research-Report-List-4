<p><p>LNG Marine Pumps are classified into three main types: Spray Pumps, Cargo Pumps, and Others. Spray Pumps are used for spraying LNG into the atmosphere in order to prevent vapor build-up. Cargo Pumps are used to transfer LNG from storage tanks to the ship's engines. Other types of pumps in the market may include ballast pumps, fire pumps, and emergency pumps which serve various purposes in LNG marine operations. Each type of pump plays a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient transportation of LNG on marine vessels.</p></p>
