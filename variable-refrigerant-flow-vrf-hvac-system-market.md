<p><p>Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) HVAC systems are popular in the market due to their flexibility and energy efficiency. There are two main types of VRF systems: 2 pipe systems and 3 pipe systems. </p><p>2 pipe systems use the same pipe to carry the refrigerant to the indoor units for both cooling and heating, causing them to switch between functions. In contrast, 3 pipe systems have separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing for simultaneous operation. Both systems offer different advantages and are suited for different types of buildings and applications.</p></p>
