Linear actuators are designed to create linear motion by converting hydraulic energy into mechanical motion in a straight line. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, are designed to create rotary motion by converting hydraulic energy into mechanical motion in a circular direction. Both types of actuators are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation for precise control and movement of machinery and equipment.</p></p>
