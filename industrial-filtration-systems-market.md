<p><p>Industrial Filtration Systems Market includes two main types - Skid Mounted and Portable systems. Skid Mounted systems are fixed to a platform or skid and are specifically designed for processing large volumes of fluids. They are often used in stationary industrial applications. Portable systems, on the other hand, are designed for easy mobility and are used for on-the-go filtration needs. They are typically smaller in size and are ideal for temporary or remote locations where a stationary system may not be practical.</p></p>
