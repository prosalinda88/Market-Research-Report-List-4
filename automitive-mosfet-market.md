<p><strong>The Automitive MOSFET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N-Channel</li><li>P-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive MOSFET market consists of two main types: N-Channel and P-Channel. N-Channel MOSFETs are known for their superior performance and efficiency, making them widely used in automotive applications. They have low ON resistance and high current-carrying capability. On the other hand, P-Channel MOSFETs are used in applications where positive voltage is required. They have higher resistance and lower current-carrying capability compared to N-Channel MOSFETs. Both types play a crucial role in powering and controlling electrical systems in vehicles.</p></p>
