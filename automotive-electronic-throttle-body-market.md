<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Throttle Body market consists of three main types: Actuator, Throttle Plate, and Throttle Position Sensor. The actuator controls the opening and closing of the throttle valve, while the throttle plate regulates the airflow into the engine. The throttle position sensor detects the position of the throttle plate and sends this information to the engine control unit. These components work together to ensure efficient combustion and optimal performance in modern electronic throttle systems.</p></p>
