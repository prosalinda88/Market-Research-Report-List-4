<p><p>The Liquid Effluent Monitors market can be divided into two main types: Off-Line and On-Line monitors. On the other hand, On-Line monitors are permanently installed and provide real-time monitoring and analysis of liquid effluents as they are being discharged. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring that liquid effluents meet regulatory standards and help industries maintain environmental compliance.</p></p>
