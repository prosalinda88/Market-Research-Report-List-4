<p><p>Metal cleaning machines are used in various industries for removing dirt, oil, grease, and other contaminants from metal surfaces. There are two main types of metal cleaning machines on the market: solvent metal cleaning machines and aqueous solution metal cleaning machines. Solvent metal cleaning machines use solvents such as mineral spirits or acetone to clean metal surfaces, whereas aqueous solution metal cleaning machines use water-based solutions that are environmentally friendly and safe for workers. Both types of machines are essential for ensuring clean and finished metal products in industrial settings.</p></p>
