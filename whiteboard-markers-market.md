<p><p>Whiteboard markers are available in two main types: Dry Erase and Wet Erase. Dry Erase markers are most commonly used on whiteboards and erase easily with a dry cloth or eraser. They are suitable for everyday use in offices, classrooms, and presentations. Wet Erase markers, on the other hand, are designed for more permanent writing on surfaces like laminated calendars or menu boards, requiring a damp cloth to be wiped off. Both types offer a range of colors and tip sizes to suit different needs.</p></p>
