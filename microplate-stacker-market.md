<p><p>Microplate stackers are laboratory automation devices used to move microplates in and out of instruments. There are three main types of microplate stackers based on their throughput capacity: low-throughput, medium-throughput, and high-throughput. Low-throughput stackers are designed for small-scale research labs with minimal plate processing needs. Medium-throughput stackers are suitable for moderate plate processing volumes in mid-sized labs. High-throughput stackers are capable of handling large quantities of plates in high-volume laboratories, making them ideal for high-throughput screening and testing applications.</p></p>
