<p><p>Nickel electroplating additives refer to chemicals used in the process of nickel electroplating to improve the overall quality and performance of the coating. The market can be categorized into three main types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents are used to prepare the surface for electroplating, electroplating additives enhance the deposition process and improve the final finish, while post-treatment agents are applied after electroplating to improve the durability and appearance of the coating.</p></p>
