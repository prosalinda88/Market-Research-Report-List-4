<p><p>GC syringes are used in gas chromatography to inject samples into the gas chromatograph. There are two main types of GC syringes: Autosampler syringes and manual syringes. Autosampler syringes are used with automated sampling systems that allow for multiple samples to be analyzed quickly and efficiently. Manual syringes are operated by hand and are used when precise control over sample injection is required. Both types of syringes play a crucial role in the accurate and efficient analysis of samples in gas chromatography.</p></p>
