<p><strong>The Urea Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large Granule Urea</li><li>Small Granule Urea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large granule urea and small granule urea are two types of urea products categorized based on the size of the granules. Large granule urea is typically used for agricultural applications due to its slow-release properties and reduced risk of nutrient leaching. It is also favored for bulk blending with other fertilizers. On the other hand, small granule urea is preferred for foliar application and use in greenhouses or hydroponic systems due to its fast-acting nature and ease of application. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the agricultural industry.</p></p>
