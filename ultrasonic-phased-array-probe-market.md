<p><p>Ultrasonic phased array probes are categorized into three main types based on their design and functionality: straight probes, angle probes, and curvature probes. Straight probes are used for conducting inspections in a linear path. Angle probes are designed to inspect components at various angles without the need to physically rotate the probe. Curvature probes are utilized for inspecting curved surfaces and complex geometries. Each type of probe offers unique features and benefits for specific inspection requirements in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and oil & gas.</p></p>
