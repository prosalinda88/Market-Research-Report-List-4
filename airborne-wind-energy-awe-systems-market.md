<p><p>Airborne Wind Energy (AWE) Systems utilize various types of aerial devices to harness wind energy for power generation. These include kites, lifting balloons, and drones. Kite-based systems use large, maneuverable kites to capture wind energy at high altitudes. Lifting balloons are helium-filled aerostats attached to a wind turbine that can reach heights where wind speeds are more consistent. Drones equipped with turbines can be deployed to capture wind energy in hard-to-reach or remote locations. These AWE systems offer efficient and sustainable alternatives to traditional wind turbines.</p></p>
