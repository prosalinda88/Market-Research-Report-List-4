<p><p>Automotive window regulators are categorized into different types based on their mechanism of operation. The Scissor Regulator uses a scissor-like action to raise and lower the window. The Cable Regulator utilizes cables to move the window up and down smoothly. The Flexible Shaft Regulator uses a flexible shaft to control the window movement. Other types of regulators include those that use gears, pulleys, or electric motors. Each type of regulator offers unique benefits and features to meet the diverse needs of the automotive market.</p></p>
