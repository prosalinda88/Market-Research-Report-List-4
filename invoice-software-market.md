<p><p>Invoice software comes in two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based invoice software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering benefits such as easy accessibility, flexibility, and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises invoice software is installed and maintained on the company's own servers, providing greater control and security over data. Each market type caters to different business needs and preferences, with cloud-based solutions often favored for their convenience and cost-effectiveness, while on-premises solutions are chosen for their security and customization options.</p></p>
