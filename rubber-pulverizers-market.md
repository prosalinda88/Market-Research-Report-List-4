<p><p>Rubber pulverizers are divided into three main types based on the size of the final product they produce. Coarse pulverizers are used for recycling and tire production, fine pulverizers are used for creating rubber compounds, and micro pulverizers are utilized in industries such as pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.</p></p>
