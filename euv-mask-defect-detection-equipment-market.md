<p><p>The EUV Mask Defect Detection Equipment market includes two main types: Chip and Chip Model, and Chip and Database Model. In the Chip and Chip Model, the equipment directly compares the mask and the wafer to detect defects. In the Chip and Database Model, the equipment compares the mask with a pre-loaded database of known defects to identify any anomalies. Both models play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of EUV masks used in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
