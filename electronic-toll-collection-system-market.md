<p><strong>The Electronic Toll Collection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems are categorized into four main types. RFID-based ETC systems utilize Radio Frequency Identification technology for automatic toll collection. DSRC-based ETC systems use Dedicated Short Range Communication for efficient toll collection. Video analytic-based ETC systems use video cameras for vehicle identification and toll collection. GNSS/GPS-based ETC systems use Global Navigation Satellite System technology for accurate toll collection. Each type of ETC system offers unique features and benefits for seamless and convenient toll collection on highways and expressways.</p></p>
