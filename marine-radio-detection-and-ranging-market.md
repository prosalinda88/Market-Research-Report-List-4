<p><p>Marine Radio Detection And Ranging (RADAR) technology for ship navigation can be categorized into two types based on the frequency bands it operates in: X Band Radars and S Band Radars. X Band Radars operate in the frequency range of 8-12 GHz, providing high resolution and accuracy for short to medium-range detection. On the other hand, S Band Radars operate in the frequency range of 2-4 GHz, offering longer detection range capabilities. Both types of radars are essential tools for safe navigation and collision avoidance at sea.</p></p>
