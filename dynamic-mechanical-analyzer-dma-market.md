<p><p>Dynamic Mechanical Analyzer (DMA) is a testing instrument used to measure the mechanical properties of materials under dynamic conditions. Forced Resonance Analyzers apply a forced oscillation to the sample, while Free Resonance Analyzers measure the natural resonant frequency of the material. Forced Resonance Analyzers are more common and provide controlled and consistent testing conditions, while Free Resonance Analyzers are used for specific applications where measuring natural frequencies is crucial. Both types of analyzers play a significant role in characterizing the mechanical behavior of materials.</p></p>
