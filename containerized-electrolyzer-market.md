<p><p>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer and PEM Electrolyzer are two main types of containerized electrolyzer markets. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers use potassium hydroxide as an electrolyte and have been in use for many years, offering low cost and high efficiency. On the other hand, PEM Electrolyzers use a polymer electrolyte membrane and offer higher efficiency, fast response times, and greater flexibility in operation. Both types are essential in the market for containerized electrolyzers, catering to different needs and applications.</p></p>
