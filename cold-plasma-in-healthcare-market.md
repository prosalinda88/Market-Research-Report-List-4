<p><p>Cold plasma in healthcare refers to the use of ionized gas with low temperatures to treat various medical conditions. There are two main types of cold plasma technologies used in the healthcare market- low-pressure cold plasma and atmospheric cold plasma. Low-pressure cold plasma operates at near-vacuum conditions and is typically used for disinfection and sterilization purposes. On the other hand, atmospheric cold plasma operates at ambient pressure and is often used for wound healing, cancer treatment, and other medical applications.</p></p>
