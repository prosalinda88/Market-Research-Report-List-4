<p><ul><li>Air-Operated Diaphragm Pump</li><li>Electrically Operated Diaphragm Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm dry pumps can be divided into two main types: air-operated diaphragm pumps and electrically operated diaphragm pumps. Air-operated diaphragm pumps utilize compressed air to move the diaphragm and create suction, making them suitable for use in environments where electricity is not readily available. On the other hand, electrically operated diaphragm pumps are powered by electricity and are ideal for applications where a constant and reliable power source is necessary. Both types of diaphragm pumps offer efficient and reliable pumping solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
