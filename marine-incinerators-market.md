<p><ul><li>Horizontal Marine Incinerators</li><li>Vertical Cyclone Marine Incinerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine incinerators are used for the disposal of waste generated on ships. Horizontal marine incinerators are designed with a horizontal cylindrical chamber, while vertical cyclone marine incinerators have a vertical design with a cyclone burner for more efficient combustion. Horizontal incinerators are typically used for solid waste disposal, while vertical cyclone incinerators are preferred for burning liquid and sludge waste. Both types of incinerators are essential for maintaining cleanliness and environmental compliance on marine vessels.</p></p>
