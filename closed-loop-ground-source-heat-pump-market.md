<p><p>Closed-Loop Ground Source Heat Pump systems are classified into three main types based on the heat exchange method: Horizontal Ground Source Heat Pump, Vertical Ground Source Heat Pump, and Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump. Horizontal systems utilize loops buried horizontally in the ground, vertical systems use deep vertical boreholes, and surface water systems extract heat from bodies of water. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different geological and hydrological conditions, providing efficient and sustainable heating and cooling solutions for buildings.</p></p>
