<p><p>Plastic pellet dryers can be categorized into two main types: batch dryers and continuous dryers. Batch dryers require the pellets to be loaded into the dryer in batches and dried before unloading, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations. Continuous dryers, on the other hand, allow for a constant flow of pellets through the drying process, making them more efficient and suitable for larger-scale production. Both types of dryers play a crucial role in the plastic pellet drying process, catering to different production needs.</p></p>
