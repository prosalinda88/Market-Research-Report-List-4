<p><ul><li>Rotary Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li><li>Reciprocating Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps comprise two main types: Rotary Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps and Reciprocating Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps. Rotary pumps operate by continuously displacing fluid in a rotating motion, while reciprocating pumps displace fluid through a back-and-forth motion. Rotary pumps are commonly used for high-viscosity liquids, while reciprocating pumps are suited for high-pressure applications. Both types are crucial in various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics, for their hygienic and efficient fluid transfer capabilities.</p></p>
