<p><p>Vacuum Insulated Piping (VIP) is a type of piping system designed to efficiently transport cryogenic fluids while minimizing heat transfer and maintaining low temperatures. VIP consists of an inner pipe carrying the cryogenic fluid, surrounded by a vacuum space to insulate the pipe and reduce heat transfer. This technology is widely used in industries such as healthcare, electronics, and energy, where precise temperature control is crucial.</p><p>The VIP Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period.
