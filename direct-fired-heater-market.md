<p><p>Direct Fired Heater market is categorized into three main types based on their heating mechanisms: Radiant Heater, Convection Heater, and Radiant-Convection Heater. Radiant Heaters emit infrared radiation that directly heats objects and people in the vicinity. Convection Heaters work by heating the air around them, which then circulates to warm up the room. Radiant-Convection Heaters combine both methods to provide quick and efficient heating. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
