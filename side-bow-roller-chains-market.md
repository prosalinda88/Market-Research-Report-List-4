<p><p>Side bow roller chains are used in applications where the chain operates on sprockets with side loads. Single-pitch side bow roller chains have rollers with the same pitch as the link plates, while double-pitch side bow roller chains have rollers with twice the pitch of the link plates. Single-pitch chains are suitable for most standard applications, while double-pitch chains are used in instances where longer pitches are required. Both types offer flexibility and smooth operation in environments with side loads.</p></p>
