<p><p>Cleanroom airlock systems are essential components in preventing contamination in controlled environments. There are several types of airlock systems available, including Cascading Airlock, which uses a series of chambers to create a buffer zone, Bubble Airlock, which uses a single chamber with positive pressure to keep contaminants out, Sink Airlock, which uses negative pressure to remove contaminants from personnel entering the cleanroom, and Other airlock systems with unique designs to suit specific cleanroom requirements. Each type of airlock system plays a critical role in maintaining the cleanliness and integrity of cleanroom environments.</p></p>
