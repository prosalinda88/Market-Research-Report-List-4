<p><strong>The 5-Axis Water-Jet Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Water-Jet Cutter</li><li>Abrasive Water-Jet Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pure Water-Jet Cutter operates by using a high-pressure jet of water to cut through materials, such as foam, rubber, or soft metals, without the need for abrasives. On the other hand, Abrasive Water-Jet Cutter combines water with abrasive materials, such as garnet or aluminum oxide, to cut through hard materials like steel, glass, or stone. Both types of water-jet cutters offer precise and efficient cutting solutions for a wide range of industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
