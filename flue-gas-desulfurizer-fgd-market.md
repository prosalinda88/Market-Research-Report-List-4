<p><p>Flue Gas Desulfurizer (FGD) market includes three main types: Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer, Semi-Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer, and Wet Flue Gas Desulfurizer. Dry FGD systems use a dry sorbent to remove sulfur dioxide from flue gas, while semi-dry systems combine both dry and wet processes. Wet FGD systems use a liquid scrubbing agent to remove sulfur dioxide. Each type of FGD system has its own advantages and limitations, depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
