<p><p>The car strut market includes various types such as coilover struts, gas charged struts, and pneumatic struts. Coilover struts feature a coil spring wrapped around the shock absorber, providing adjustability in ride height and stiffness. Gas charged struts use nitrogen gas to provide a smoother and more controlled ride. Pneumatic struts utilize compressed air for suspension adjustment, offering a customizable driving experience. These different types cater to varying preferences and needs of car owners looking to improve their vehicle's handling and performance.</p></p>
